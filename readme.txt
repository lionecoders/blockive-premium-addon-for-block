Essential collection of premium Gutenberg blocks for WordPress. Supercharge your website with beautifully designed, highly customizable, and performance-optimized blocks.

== Description ==

**lc-immeasurable-block-widgets-collection** is a premium collection of Gutenberg blocks designed to help you create stunning websites without any coding knowledge. Whether you are building a landing page, a portfolio, or a business website, these blocks provide the flexibility and styling options you need to stand out.

Built with performance in mind, our blocks are lightweight and load only the necessary assets, ensuring your site remains fast and responsive.

### Included Blocks:

1.  **LC Accordion**: Create beautiful, collapsible content sections with advanced styling options for titles, icons, and animations.
2.  **LC Countdown Timer**: Drive urgency and increase conversions with fully customizable countdown timers for sales and events.
3.  **LC Drop Caps**: Elevate your typography with elegant drop caps that make your articles look professional and polished.
4.  **LC FAQ**: Schema-ready FAQ block that helps with SEO while providing a clean way to answer common customer questions.
5.  **LC Heading**: Design eye-catching headings with custom gradients, unique fonts, and modern typography settings.
6.  **LC Pie Chart**: Visualize your data beautifully with dynamic pie and donut charts powered by Chart.js.
7.  **LC Pricing Table**: Display your service plans or products in a clean, high-conversion pricing comparison table.
8.  **LC Progress Bar**: Show off your skills or project progress with animated and highly customizable progress bars.
9.  **LC Social Icons**: Add stylish social media links to your pages with custom colors, shapes, and hover effects.
10. **LC Tabs**: Organize large amounts of content into sleek, responsive tab systems to improve user experience.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/lc-block-widgets` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. You can now find the "LC Widgets" category in your Gutenberg block inserter.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Are these blocks responsive? =
Yes, all blocks are designed to be fully responsive and look great on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

= Can I customize the colors and fonts? =
Absolutely! Each block comes with extensive styling options in the sidebar, allowing you to match your brand's look and feel perfectly.

= Does it work with any theme? =
Yes, lc-immeasurable-block-widgets-collection is designed to work seamlessly with any standard WordPress theme that supports the Gutenberg editor.
